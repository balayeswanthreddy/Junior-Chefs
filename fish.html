Ingredients:

  • Fish fillets-500 grams
  • Lemon juice - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Rice flour- 2-3 tablespoons
  • Oil for frying
       ->Marinating the fish:
        1.Wash the fish fillets and pat them dry with paper towels.
        2.In a bowl,mix lemonjuice,salt,chilipowder,turmericpowder, 
          ginger-garlic paste,corianderpowder,cuminpowder,and garammasala 
          powder to make a marinade.
        3.Apply this marinade all over the fish fillets, ensuring they are 
          evenly coated. Let the fish marinate for at least 30 minutes to 
          1 hour in the refrigerator.
       ->Coating the fish:
        1.After marination,sprinkle rice flour or all-purpose flour over 
          the marinated fish fillets.This will help in forming a crispy 
          coating when frying.
        2.Gently coat the fish fillets with the flour on both sides, 
          shaking off any excess flour. 
       ->Frying the fish:
        1.Heat oil in a frying pan or skillet over medium heat. Make sure 
          the oilis hot enough before adding the fish.
        2.Carefully place the coated fish fillets into the hot oil. Do not 
          overcrowd the pan; fry in batches if necessary.
        3.Fry the fish for about 3-4 minutes until they turn golden 
          brown and crispy.
        4.Once done,remove the fried fish from the oil and place them on a  
          platelined with paper towels to drain excess oil. 
        5.Serve the hot and crispy fish fry
